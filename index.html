FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Don't see yours below — call us directly!

How much does home shifting cost? +
The cost depends on the distance, volume of items, packing requirements, and floor access. Local shifting starts around ₹3,000–₹8,000 and interstate shifting varies based on destination. Call us for a free no-obligation quote!
Do you provide insurance for goods during transit? +
Yes, we offer optional goods-in-transit insurance for all moves. We strongly recommend it for valuable items, electronics, and antiques. Ask our team for details when you request a quote.
Is packing and unpacking included in the service? +
Yes! Our standard packages include professional packing and unpacking services. We use bubble wrap, corrugated sheets, stretch film, and strong boxes. Unpacking and arrangement at the new location is also available.
How early should I book my move? +
We recommend booking at least 5–7 days in advance for local moves and 10–15 days for interstate moves. However, we can accommodate urgent same-day moves as well — just call us!
Do you move outside Maharashtra? +
Absolutely! We provide interstate relocation services across India — Mumbai to Delhi, Pune to Bangalore, Kalyan to Hyderabad, and more. We cover all major cities and states with GPS-tracked trucks.
Do you transport cars and bikes? +
Yes! We specialize in car and bike transportation using enclosed and open carriers. Your vehicle is secured with padded straps and delivered damage-free to any destination in India.
What items do you NOT transport? +
We do not transport perishables, flammable materials, hazardous chemicals, or illegal substances. For pets, please arrange separate transportation. Contact us for any special items that need clarification.
Can I track my shipment in real time? +
Yes! All our vehicles are fitted with GPS trackers. Once your consignment is dispatched, we'll share the tracking link with you via WhatsApp so you can monitor the journey in real time.
